# Stoubridge

## Route

The Snow Hill Lines is a collective name for the local services running through Birmingham Snow Hill station. Most services are operated by West Midlands Railway. Tis simulation covers the section of route between Rowley Regis and Kidderminster, where half the services terminate. The busy junction station of Stourbridge lies in the middle of this section, servicing as the interchange point to the Stourbridge Branch Shuttle - a 6 train per hour service between the Junction and Stourbridge Town station, operated by the UK's smallest trains - the Class 139 "Parry People Movers".

## Simulation

A timetable representing Wednesday 11th March has been included, with all West Midlands Railway passenger services. The off peak service pattern is as follows:
- 1tph Stratford-upon-Avon to Worcester Foregate Street
- 1tph Stratford-upon-Avon to Kidderminster
- 1tph Whitlocks End to Kidderminster
- 1tph Dorridge to Worcester Foregate Street
- 6tph Stourbridge Junction to Stourbridge Town

There are also several Chiltern Railways and CrossCountry services at the beginning and end of service, along with freight and test trains throughout the day.

*Please note: This timetable requires v2.23.0 or later of RailOS to run, as it uses the Minimum Dwell Times feature.*

## Operational Tips

- Try not to set routes across level crossings too early - however ideally trains should always be "running on greens".
- Don't forget the Stourbridge branch - it runs very frequently and can easily be missed, resulting in delays throughout the day.
- Be careful with routes at Kidderminster and Stourbridge - terminating trains reversing and freight trains can appear in unexpected orders.
